Clutch

Welcome to Clutch, the most intimate shopping experience ever. We work closely with stores to provide a personalized retail shopping experience by presenting you with custom coupons and great deals!

How does it work?

When you enter a store, Clutch recognizes that you have arrived and automatically serves you current deals. As you continue browsing, Clutch makes use of iBeacon technology to provide you with custom coupons that are just for you! These are deals that retailers have picked just for you based on your interests and your browsing history. What a clutch deal!
